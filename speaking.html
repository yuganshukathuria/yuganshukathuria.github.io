Speaking & advisory

Helping senior teams think clearly about technology.

I work with leadership teams in settings built for decisions, not spectacle. The aim is always the same: to help a group of senior people see a complex technology question clearly and align on what to do next.

How I work with teams

Formats

Executive briefings

Focused sessions for leadership teams on a specific question — the state of enterprise AI, what a data platform really requires, or how to measure a transformation. Grounded, candid and tailored to the organisation's context.

Leadership workshops

Working sessions that move a team from discussion to decision, structured to surface the real trade-offs and leave the room with a shared view and clear next steps.

Client sessions

Advisory conversations with senior stakeholders, framing technology decisions in business terms and helping leaders weigh options with confidence.

Internal knowledge sharing

Sessions for technology and delivery teams on the practices behind dependable transformation — from data product thinking to delivery discipline.

Suggested topics

Themes I am often asked to address

  • From experiments to operations: making enterprise AI dependable
  • Data platforms that earn trust across the business
  • Measuring the value of data and AI investment
  • Cloud modernisation without importing yesterday's complexity
  • Leading transformation when the outcome is uncertain
  • Operating models for data and platform teams at scale

Sessions are shaped around your organisation's situation. If a topic here is close to what you need, it can be adapted; if it is not listed, it can usually be built.
